Kiwi Straberry Smoothie

This recipe was obtained from allrecipes.com, it is a yummy tropical style smoothie made with fruits like bananas, kiwis, and strawberries.

Ingridients:

  • 3 quarters of a cup of pineapple and orange juice blend
  • 1 half of a cup of vanilla frozen yogurt
  • 1 banana
  • 6 strawberries
  • 1 kiwi

Directions

Step 1:

Place juice blend, frozen yogurt, banana, strawberries, and kiwi into a blender. Blend until smooth and pour into 2 glasses
