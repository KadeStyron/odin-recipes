Strawberry Banana Protein Smoothie

This recipe was obtained from allrecipes.com it is a protein dense smoothie that contains almonds and protein powder.

Ingredients:

  • 1 and a quarter cups of fresh strawberries
  • 1 banana
  • 10 whole almonds
  • 2 tablespoons of water
  • 1 cup ice cubes
  • 3 tablespoons chocolate flavored protein powder

Directions

Step 1:

Place strawberries, banana, almonds, and water into a blender; blend to combine.

Step 2:

Add ice cubes and puree until smooth

Step 3:

Add protein powder and mix until evenly incorporated, about 30 seconds.

Note:

This is only one serving, make sure to multiply for more servings.
